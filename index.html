Our Vision

Oakleon is creating a new kind of data architecture, and we’d like you to be a part of it. We think businesses should be able to adapt to a changing world on the fly. If unforeseen types of information, customer behaviors, and disruptive business models arise, a company should have a system that can immediately shape itself to match. Your application should fit itself to the way you do business, not the other way around. And companies shouldn’t need a computer expert for this-- it should be intuitive and simple. Oakleon is looking for developers to work with us in order to make this a reality. Check out the details below.

Our Team

Tim Cash leads the engineering effort at Oakleon. He spent ten years in the Intelligence Community and six years in the Air Force heading up analytic, operational, and development teams. He’s delivered products like web applications used for national investigations and 3D visualisation software for geospatial analysis. Now he wants this level of technology to be available to everyone. While consulting for the government, Tim pushed for open access to information and open source software. He has a degree in applied mathematics.

Jason Walton leads Oakleon’s business development. He’s founded 15 pest control companies with 22 branch locations in 8 different states, including Moxie Pest Control, Homefront Pest Control, Joshua's Pest Control, and Mayday Pest Control, with a combined value over $60 million in annual revenue. Prior to service industry ownership, Jason worked with Eclipse Marketing, and his degree is in accounting.
