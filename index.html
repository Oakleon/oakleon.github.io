OAKLEON

Evolving Information

Oakleon is creating a new kind of data architecture, and we’d like you to be a part of it. We think businesses should be able to adapt to a changing world on the fly. If unforeseen types of information, customer behaviors, and disruptive business models arise, a company should have a system that can immediately shape itself to match. Your application should fit itself to the way you do business, not the other way around. And companies shouldn’t need a computer expert for this-- it should be intuitive and simple. Oakleon is looking for developers to work with us in order to make this a reality.

Team

Tim Cash
Co-Founder

Tim is leading the engineering effort at Oakleon. Before Oakleon he spent six years in the Air Force and ten years in the Intelligence Community heading up analytic, operational, and development teams. He has delieved a number of products ranging from web application used for national investigations to 3D visualisation software for geospatial analysis. While consulting for the government he pushed for more open access to information and expanded use of open source software. Tim holds a B.S. in Applied Mathematics.
